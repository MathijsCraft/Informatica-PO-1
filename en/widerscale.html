Medical wordld

Medical Information Technology (Embedded systems within the medical sector) is increasingly being used in healthcare facilities. Safe use and implementation of this technology requires measures, measures to ensure that the risks are as small as possible. This is the only way to guarantee durability, reliability and patient safety. This raises a host of practical issues. In this article, we address these in detail, using examples and explanations. We cover not only software applications related to medical information technology, but also embedded software on medical devices and medical systems, as well as the associated network infrastructure and hardware.


When is medical information technology considered a medical device? To be determined as a medical device, medical information technology must meet several requirements of the Medical Device Act. The definition of a medical device is as follows: 'any instrument, device or apparatus, software or substance or other article used alone or in combination, including software specifically intended by the manufacturer to be used for diagnostic and/or therapeutic purposes and required for its proper functioning, intended by the manufacturer to be used in humans for:
Diagnose, preventie, bewaking, behandeling of verlichting van ziekten; Diagnose, bewaking, behandeling, verlichting of compensatie van verwondingen of een handicap; Onderzoek naar of vervanging of wijziging van de anatomie of van een fysiologisch proces; Beheersing van de bevruchting, waarbij de belangrijkste beoogde werking in of aan het menselijk lichaam niet met farmacologische of immunologische middelen of door metabolisme wordt bereikt, maar wel door dergelijke middelen kan worden ondersteund.’ (Bron: Medical Device Directive (MDD), Medical Device Regulation (MDR)) Dit vertelt ons dat zolang medische informatietechnologie is bedoeld voor patiëntenzorg en/of onderzoek naar ziekten en handicaps mag je dit een medisch hulpmiddel noemen. Embedded systemen worden veel gebruikt in de Medische wereld, er zijn hele bedrijven die zichzelf speciaal richten op het ontwerpen en maken van Embedded Systemen voor de medische wereld. Hiervoor zijn enorm veel wetten en regelgevingen waar ontwerpers rekening mee moeten houden en daarom duurt dit ook vaak erg lang. In the military

TAccess to the latest technology is essential in the military and defense industry. Most of today's advanced technology is run on computers and embedded systems to support the unique requirements of the military. The general embedded systems used in consumer applications are not the same as the embedded systems used in military applications. Systems used in the military must be extremely secure, reliable and powerful. Military use of embedded and computer systems is very different than for consumers. Military systems must be more efficient, reliable, secure and robust than consumer systems. They are required to meet the very highest levels of security to make it more difficult to intercept data or do something called reverse engineering, which means you try to deduce from the product how something works and use it for your own benefit. To make sure this all goes well, reliable components from reliable manufacturers are used. Governments therefore also give a significant portion of their annual budget to military embedded systems to ensure national security.

Politie

De Politie maakt veer gebruik van ingebedde systemen denk bijvoorbeeld aan beveiligingscamera's en mobiele apparatuur.

Sinds eind 2019 hebben alle agenten in New York City een zo genoemde "bodycam" ten alle tijden bij zich. Dit is eigenlijk gewoon een camera die alles opneemt wat de agent ziet of doet. Sinds de introductie van dit zijn er agenten ontslagen in verschillende steden in Amerika nadat corruptie tot het licht was gekomen door deze bodycams. Ook is de hoeveelheid klachten vanwege politie wangedrag met bijna 45 procent verminderd. (Cawley, 2022)
